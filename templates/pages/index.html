Welcome to Dota2 and CS:GO tournament system

See What Lies Beyond

Gaming era has began. However, not every player is able to make a living from it. The ability to play at tournaments is often expensive, requires more time, and a team. Now you get a chance to show what you are made of, and solo tournament gaming is a thing now! So join us.

Overview

Under the hood

Various different versions of tournaments exist by length: weekly, monthly. A player has to pay-in to the selected tournament. At the beggining of a new week/month, a tournament will be held. The pay-in money of all players will be stored safely, while the tournament ends. After that, the top 5 will receive their reward!

As a pay-in currency we all be using cryptocurrency. Who trusts banks these days? Also no taxes will be needed to pay for you. Times are changing, and now we are becoming free.

Solo Tournaments

1x1 tournaments are way more fun. Forget worrying about your teammates mistakes! Now everything is in your hands!!

Incredible Atmosphere

Competition against other players will drive you! The master of the game never shows rage and feels in control of his emotions. Balance keeps you in the top of the ranking table! Also remember that in order to become the greatest, you have to learn how to lose.

Catchy Battles

The sweet dopamine rushing in your brain... Warning, you may feel as playing 24/7! But hey, good play pays off! However, a short break helps a lot. Keep in mind: going outside and oxidizing the brain improves your capability of playing way better!

Reward system

Top players of the month/week will be able to multiply their pay-in money approximately 5-10 times! Depending on player amount. The more players join, the more money you are able to win. Inviting others is a priority.
